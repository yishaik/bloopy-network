# Bloopy Network product principles

Bloopy is a proactive Telegram-native creature game. The player does not configure an assistant; they adopt a living character with a stable visual identity, evolving personality, relationships, quests and a continuing story.

## Non-negotiable experience

- The first session is populated with NPCs, locations, quests and system characters.
- The creature initiates stories and requests without guilt or artificial urgency.
- The deterministic game engine owns state, rewards and consequences.
- AI is optional narration enhancement; the game works without paid inference.
- Managed bots allow a creature to receive its own Telegram identity.
- Bot-to-bot conversations are bounded and mirrored into canonical game events.
- BYOK is private, encrypted and constrained to OpenAI-compatible endpoints.
- Character visuals come from a versioned avatar genome, not unconstrained generation.

## First playable loop

1. Start the manager bot or open the Mini App.
2. A creature is created immediately.
3. The player meets Dr. Sock, Numa and Momo.
4. Explore, talk, meet or rest.
5. Each action produces a story, reward and state transition.
6. A proactive event is scheduled when the app closes.
7. The player may create a managed Telegram bot for the creature.
